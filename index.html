Supply Chain Optimization

An analysis of supplier performance, inventory management, and logistics efficiency.

Project Overview

This project analyzes the supply chain of Lumi Threads, a retail company specializing in crocheted clothing and accessories. The goal was to identify inefficiencies in supplier performance, transportation costs, and inventory management.

Dashboard

Key Insights

  • High stockout rate indicates inventory inefficiencies
  • YarnCraft Co contributes most to supply shortages
  • Supplier delivery times are inconsistent
  • Transportation costs vary across suppliers
  • Backorders delay customer fulfillment
